CHUNK_ID: SRH-008
TOPIC: Carpet Area vs Super Built-Up Area Explanation
TAGS: carpet area, super built-up area, loading factor, area calculation

At Sunrise Heights, the carpet area is calculated as per RERA guidelines and refers to the actual usable floor area within the walls of the apartment, excluding the thickness of walls, balconies, and common areas. The super built-up area (SBA) includes the carpet area plus proportional share of common areas such as lobbies, staircases, lift shafts, and amenity spaces. The loading factor at Sunrise Heights is approximately 28% for 1 BHK and 2 BHK units and 27% for 3 BHK units. Per RERA norms, all pricing and sale agreements are based on the carpet area only, not the super built-up area.
